📋 Remember the Sentence Order in English:

🔵 Subject + Verb + Object: "I play football."

🟠 Subject + am/is/are + wearing + clothes: "She is wearing a red shirt."

🟢 There is/are + noun + place: "There is a cat in the garden."

🟣 Numbers: "Fifteen is smaller than fifty."
